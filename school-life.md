<section class="section">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-header">
      <span class="section-label">Highlights</span>
      <h2>Life at Bocskai School</h2>
      <p>
        Beyond the classroom, school life at Bocskai is full of memorable experiences —
        cultural celebrations, excursions, competitions and community events that bring
        students and families together.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="grid-3">

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
          <div class="card-icon">🇭🇺</div>
          <h3>Cultural Celebrations</h3>
          <p>
            We celebrate major Hungarian cultural events throughout the year — including
            National Day, Easter traditions, the Harvest Festival, and St Nicholas Day.
            Students learn the significance of these celebrations through song, craft and storytelling.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
          <div class="card-icon">⛺</div>
          <h3>Camping Excursions</h3>
          <p>
            Our annual camping trips are a highlight of the school calendar. Students bond
            with classmates, sing Hungarian folk songs around the campfire and build
            lasting friendships in a relaxed outdoor setting.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
          <div class="card-icon">📚</div>
          <h3>Bookworm Competition</h3>
          <p>
            Our popular bookworm reading competition encourages students to read as many
            Hungarian books as possible. Students earn recognition and prizes for their
            reading achievements across the year.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
          <div class="card-icon">🎭</div>
          <h3>Performances &amp; Concerts</h3>
          <p>
            Students regularly perform for parents and the broader community — reciting
            poems, performing Hungarian folk dances and singing traditional songs at
            end-of-year concerts and community events.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
          <div class="card-icon">🏛️</div>
          <h3>Special Guests &amp; Visits</h3>
          <p>
            We have hosted distinguished guests including the Hungarian President and
            various cultural dignitaries, giving students an inspiring connection to
            Hungary and the broader Hungarian-Australian community.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card">
        <div class="card-body">
          <div class="card-icon">🎨</div>
          <h3>Arts &amp; Crafts</h3>
          <p>
            Hungarian folk art traditions — including embroidery patterns, painted eggs,
            paper cutting and pottery — are woven throughout our curriculum, connecting
            students to centuries of cultural heritage.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
  </div>
</section>
