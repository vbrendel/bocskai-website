        <h2>More Than a School</h2>
        <p>
          The Bocskai Hungarian School brings together Hungarian-Australian families from
          across Melbourne who share a commitment to keeping the Hungarian language and
          culture alive for future generations.
        </p>
        <p>
          Our community is welcoming to families of all backgrounds — whether you're a
          native Hungarian speaker or just beginning to reconnect with your heritage,
          you'll find a warm home here.
        </p>
        <p>
          Beyond classes, we come together for cultural celebrations, excursions,
          community dinners and events throughout the year. Many lifelong friendships
          have been formed through our school.
        </p>
