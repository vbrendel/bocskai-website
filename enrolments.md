            We're happy to answer any questions before you commit.
          </p>
          <p>
            📧 <a href="mailto:bocskaischool@gmail.com">bocskaischool@gmail.com</a>
            &nbsp;&nbsp;
            📞 <a href="tel:0408950491">0408 950 491</a>
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="enrol-step">
        <div class="step-number">2</div>
        <div class="step-content">
          <h3>Complete the Enrolment Form</h3>
          <p>
            Once you've spoken with us, we'll send you our enrolment form. Please provide details about your child's
            age, current school year, and any prior exposure to Hungarian language.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="enrol-step">
        <div class="step-number">3</div>
        <div class="step-content">
          <h3>Class Placement</h3>
          <p>
            Based on your child's age and language background, we'll place them in the most appropriate class.
            If you're unsure, we can arrange a brief informal assessment on the first day.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="enrol-step">
        <div class="step-number">4</div>
        <div class="step-content">
          <h3>Pay School Fees</h3>
          <p>
            School fees are payable at the start of each term. We offer a family discount for multiple children.
            Partial funding assistance may be available — please enquire.
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="enrol-step">
        <div class="step-number">5</div>
        <div class="step-content">
          <h3>First Day of Classes</h3>
          <p>
            Classes run every second Sunday from 9:00am to 12:30pm at the Bocskai Community Centre,
            123 St Georges Road, Fitzroy North. We look forward to welcoming your family!
          </p>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>
  </div>
